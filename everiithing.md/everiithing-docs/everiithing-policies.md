## PRIVACY POLICY

**Last updated: June 2025**
We keep this simple. Here's exactly what we do with your information.

---

### 1. What We Collect
- **Account info** — your name, email, phone number and address when you sign up
- **Booking info** — the services you book, your location, and job details
- **Payment info** — processed securely via Flutterwave. We never store your card details
- **Device info** — browser type, IP address, and how you use the platform

---

### 2. How We Use It
- To match you with the right verified provider
- To process and confirm your bookings
- To send you updates about your job status
- To improve the platform and fix issues
- We do not sell your data. Ever.

---

### 3. Who We Share It With
- **Providers** — only the details they need to complete your job (name, location, job description)
- **Flutterwave** — to process payments securely
- **Legal authorities** — only if required by Nigerian law
- No third-party advertisers. No data brokers.

---

### 4. Your Rights
- Request a copy of your data at any time
- Ask us to delete your account and data
- Opt out of marketing emails
- Contact us at: **hello@everiithing.com**

## TERMS OF SERVICE

**Last updated: June 2025**
By using Everiithing.com, you agree to these terms. We've kept them short and honest.

---

### 1. Using Everiithing
- You must be 18 or older to use this platform
- You agree to provide accurate information when booking
- You will not misuse the platform or attempt to contact providers outside of Everiithing
- We reserve the right to suspend accounts that violate these terms

---

### 2. Bookings & Payments
- A flat ₦5,000 call-out fee is charged when you book a service
- This fee covers the provider's visit to assess the job — it is non-refundable once the provider arrives
- After assessment, your provider sends a quote. You decide whether to proceed
- Full payment is held in escrow and only released to the provider after you approve the work

---

### 3. Our Responsibilities
- We vet and verify every provider on our platform
- We do not guarantee specific outcomes but we do guarantee a verified, professional provider
- We are not liable for damages caused by misuse of the platform or inaccurate job descriptions
- If something goes wrong, contact us and we will work to resolve it fairly

---

### 4. Account Rules
- One account per person
- Keep your login details secure — you are responsible for activity on your account
- Do not create fake reviews or manipulate ratings
- Accounts found in violation will be suspended without refund

## REFUND POLICY

**Last updated: June 2025**
We believe in fair outcomes. Here's how refunds work on Everiithing.

---

### 1. Call-Out Fee
- The ₦5,000 call-out fee is **non-refundable** once a provider has been dispatched
- If you cancel before the provider is assigned, you will receive a full refund within 3–5 business days
- If you cancel after assignment but before arrival, a 50% refund applies

---

### 2. Job Disputes
- If you are unhappy with the work done, raise a dispute within 48 hours of job completion
- Do not release payment until you are satisfied with the work
- Our team will review the dispute and contact both parties within 2 business days

---

### 3. Refund Process
- Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method
- Refunds typically take 3–7 business days depending on your bank
- Escrow payments that are disputed will be held until resolution

---

### 4. Contact Us
Have a question about a refund or dispute?

- **Email:** hello@everiithing.com
- **Response time:** within 24 hours on business days
- We are committed to resolving every issue fairly and promptly
